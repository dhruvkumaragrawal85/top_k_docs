The Beyonder first appeared in the episode Arrival of Season 5 , the first chapter of that Secret Wars in the series . The Beyonder worked with Madame Web to prepare Spider-Man for the Secret Wars , supposedly to see whether good was more powerful than evil . He put Spider-Man and a small group of heroes selected by the web-slinger ( Fantastic Four , Captain America , Iron Man , Storm , and the Black Cat ) on a planet and pit them against a band of villains ( Doctor Octopus , Doctor Doom , Alistair Smythe , the Lizard , and the Red Skull ) . During this time , Doctor Doom stole the Beyonder 's powers in Doom ( the third and last chapter to the Secret Wars ) , but he was defeated , and it was implied that he had planned the whole ordeal out before it began .
