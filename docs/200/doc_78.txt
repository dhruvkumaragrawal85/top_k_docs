Common species of wildlife in Latvia include deer , wild boar , moose , lynx , bear , fox , beaver and wolves . Non-marine molluscs of Latvia include 159 species . Phytogeographically , Latvia is shared between the Central European and Northern European provinces of the Circumboreal Region within the Boreal Kingdom . According to the WWF , the territory of Latvia belongs to the ecoregion of Sarmatic mixed forests . More than half of Latvia 's territory is covered by forests , mostly Scots Pine , birch and Norway Spruce . Several species of flora and fauna are considered national symbols .
