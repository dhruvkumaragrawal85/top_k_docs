Summer starts in June and lasts until August . Summers are usually warm and sunny , with cool evenings and nights . Summers have average temperatures of around 19C 0 , with extremes of 35C 0 . Spring and autumn bring fairly mild weather . # Environment # Most of the country is composed of fertile lowland plains and moderate hills . In a typical Latvian landscape , a mosaic of vast forests alternates with fields , farmsteads , and pastures . Arable is spotted with birch groves and wooded clusters , which afford a habitat for numerous plants and animals . Latvia has hundreds of kilometres of undeveloped seashorelined by pine forests , dunes , and continuous white sand beaches . Latvia has over 12,500 rivers , which stretch for 38000 km . Major rivers include the Daugava River , Lielupe , Gauja , Venta , and Salaca , the largest spawning ground for salmon in the eastern Baltics . There are 2,256 lakes that are bigger than 1 ha , with a collective area of 1000 km2 . Mires occupy 9.9% of Latvia 's territory . Of these , 42% are raised bogs ; 49% are fens ; and 9% are transitional mires . 70% percent of the mires are untouched by civilisation , and they are a refuge for many rare species of plants and animals .
