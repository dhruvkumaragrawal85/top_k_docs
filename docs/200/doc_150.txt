Subtle physical abnormalities have been found in illnesses traditionally considered functional , such as schizophrenia . The DSM-IV-TR avoids the functional/organic distinction , and instead lists traditional psychotic illnesses , psychosis due to general medical conditions , and substance-induced psychosis . Primary psychiatric causes of psychosis include the following : * schizophrenia and schizophreniform disorder * affective ( mood ) disorders , including severe depression , and severe depression or mania in bipolar disorder ( manic depression ) .
