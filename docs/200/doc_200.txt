This cleared the way for the French army under the Duke of Berwick to besiege the imperial fort at Philippsburg , which fell after a siege of two months in July 1734 . Eugene , who was accompanied by Crown Prince Frederick of Prussia , made some attempts to relieve the siege , but never made any decisive attacks against the besieging army owing to its size and relatively poor quality of the troops under his command . Berwick was killed by a shell at Philippsburg .
