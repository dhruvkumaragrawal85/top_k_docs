In 1934 , after Einstein moved to USA , Harriet Cohen did finally play that duet concert with Einstein to raise funds to bring Jewish scientists out of Nazi Germany . Cohen and Einstein remained friends thereafter and he referred to her as the beloved piano witch . It was not until 1939 when she first met Chaim Weizmann , the future first President of Israel , that she began to support the Zionist cause and a Jewish homeland . Cohen 's 1939 visit to Palestine extended her reputation there both as a concert pianist and politically . She argued with British and Jewish officials to try to get Jewish refugees admitted on ships from Nazi Germany ( rather than be returned ) , once almost precipitating an International incident . Harriet Cohen believed passionately in a Jewish homeland but with justice to the Arab Palestinians . She survived two assassination attempts during her trip to Palestine .
