In traditional regional lore , pixies are generally benign , mischievous , short of stature and attractively childlike ; they are fond of dancing and gather outdoors in huge numbers to dance or sometimes wrestle , through the night , demonstrating parallels with the Cornish plen-an-gwary and Breton Fest Noz ( Cornish : troyl ) folk celebrations originating in the medieval period . In modern times they are usually depicted with pointed ears , and often wearing a green outfit and pointed hat . Sometimes their eyes are described as being pointed upwards at the temple ends .
