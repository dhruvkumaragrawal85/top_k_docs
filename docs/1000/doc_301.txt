The Heat Night # On August 11 , 2007 , former Heat majority owner Steve Nettleton and former Heat players were honored as part of a celebration called Remember The Heat Night hosted by the Chico Outlaws as they faced the St. George RoadRunners . The original Heat mascot Heater even made an appearance .
