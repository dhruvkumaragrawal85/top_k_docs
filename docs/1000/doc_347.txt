Naturism was a global alternative health and lifestyle movement . Naturists focused on redefining one 's life to live simply , eat cheap but nutritious vegetarian diets , and raise one 's own food if possible . The countryside was posited as a romantic alternative to urban living , and some naturists even promoted what they saw as the healthful benefits of nudism . Globally , the naturist movement counted anarchists , liberals , and socialists as its followers . However , in Cuba a particular anarchist dimension evolved led by people like Adrin del Valle , who spearheaded the Cuban effort to shift naturism 's focus away from only individual health to naturism having a social emancipatory function .
