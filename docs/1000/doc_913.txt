Members of the families of the original owners of these artworks have , in many cases , persisted in claiming title to their pre-war property . The 1964 film ' ' The Train ' ' , starring Burt Lancaster , is based on the true story of works of art which had been placed in storage for protection in France during the war , but was looted by the Germans from French museums and private art collections , to be shipped by train back to Germany . Another film , ' ' The Monuments Men ' ' ( 2014 ) , co-produced , co-written and directed by George Clooney , is based on a similar true-life story . In this film , U.S. soldiers are tasked with saving over a million pieces of art and other culturally important items throughout Europe , before their destruction by Nazi plunder .
