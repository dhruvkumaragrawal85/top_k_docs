The A30 trunk road now bypasses the town . Okehampton railway station is on the former northerly rail route from Exeter to Plymouth via Tavistock . The line from Exeter remains open for freight traffic to and from Meldon Quarry , two miles ( 3 km ) west of Okehampton . In summer , and at weekends throughout the year , the Dartmoor Railway operates a heritage railway service between Okehampton and Meldon Quarry . In 1997 , Devon County Council revived a passenger rail service from Exeter , on summer weekends only , in an attempt to reduce motor traffic to the national park . As of March 2010 there were plans to reinstate a daily service terminating in Exeter . Okehampton is served by various bus services from Exeter , Bude , Newquay and Tavistock . First Bus service X9 links from Exeter Bus station via Exeter St Davids to Okehampton and then to Bude . Other services from Exeter Bus station include the 510 Western Greyhound service via Exeter St Davids , which continues to Newquay .
