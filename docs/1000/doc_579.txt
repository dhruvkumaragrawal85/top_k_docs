Incipient brain-disease compelled him to withdraw from official life in November 1863 . He still was director of the Berlin observatory until his death on 26 August 1865 in Spandau . His successor was Wilhelm Julius Foerster . He contributed extensively to the periodical literature of astronomy . Encke 's grave is preserved at a cemetery in the Kreuzberg section of Berlin , the ' ' Friedhof II der Jerusalems- und Neuen Kirchengemeinde ' ' ( Cemetery No .
