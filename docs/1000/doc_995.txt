On the death of Areces in 1989 , his nephew , Isidoro lvarez , was named his successor and quickly became one of the most powerful men in Spain . In 1995 , El Corte Ingls bought out its only serious competitor , Galeras Preciados , which had entered bankruptcy . Stores tend to be very large in size and offer a wide range of products : stores may sell music , movies , portable and household electronics , furniture , hardware , books , clothes , groceries , gourmet food , cars and real estate . International expansion began in 2001 in Portugal , with a store in Lisbon , followed in 2006 with a store in Vila Nova de Gaia , opposite the city of Porto , and a third store is planned .
