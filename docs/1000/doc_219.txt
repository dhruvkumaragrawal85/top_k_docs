In a three-week period of conflict in Iraq during 2003 it was estimated that over 1000 tons of depleted uranium munitions were used . # Legal status in weapons # In 1996 the International Court of Justice ( ICJ ) gave an advisory opinion on the ' ' legality of the threat or use of nuclear weapons ' ' . This made it clear , in paragraphs 54 , 55 and 56 , that international law on poisonous weaponsthe Second Hague Declaration of 29 July 1899 , Hague Convention IV of 18 October 1907 and the Geneva Protocol of 17 June 1925did not cover nuclear weapons , because their prime or exclusive use was not to poison or asphyxiate .
