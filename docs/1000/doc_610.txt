The historic remoteness of this area led it to be the subject of exploration , most notably by Captain Percy Fawcett , in the quest for lost cities . It was also the rumored location of access to the interior of the earth in various Hollow Earth theories . # Demographics # Mato Grosso had a high rate of population growth in the 20th century . Despite this , the state as a whole has one of the lowest population densities of any Brazilian state . Ethnically , the state includes a relatively high proportion of caboclos ( persons of mixed European and Indian ancestry ) , as do other areas of the interior .
