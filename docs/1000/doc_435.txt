The population was 3,406 at the 2000 census . For the 2010 census , the area was split into two CDPs , Cornwells Heights and Eddington , that are adjacent unincorporated communities within Bensalem Township . Cornwells Heights is located a bit southwest of Eddington . Both communities use the Bensalem ZIP code , 19020 . The CDP was bounded on the south by Interstate 95 , on the east by Street Road , on the north and west by Hulmeville Road , and on the west by Woodhaven Road . The Philadelphia city boundary is less than a mile to the west and north . The Delaware River is located to the south-southeast . Like neighboring Andalusia , the area consists of middle-class single-family homes along a series of grid-style streets .
