It was used on religious architecture , carpets , and handwritten documents . Islamic art has reflected this balanced , harmonious world-view . It focuses on spiritual essence rather than physical form . While there has been an aversion to potential idol worship through Islamic history , this is a distinctly modern Sunni view . Persian miniatures , along with medieval depictions of Muhammad and angels in Islam , stand as prominent examples contrary to the modern Sunni tradition . Also , Shi'a Muslims are much less averse to the depiction of figures , including the Prophets as long as the depiction is respectful . # Figure representation in Islamic sacred art # The Islamic resistance to the representation of living beings @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ forms is unique to God , and it is for this reason that the role of images and image makers has been controversial.he strongest statements on the subject of figural depiction are made in the Hadith ( Traditions of the Prophet ) , where painters are challenged to breathe life into their creations and threatened with punishment on the Day of Judgment .
