The Ph.D . is generally considered to be more research oriented , while other doctorates may place more emphasis on practical applications and/or include a performance component . Such distinctions among degree types not always so clear-cut , however . For instance , most programs include traditional research training and culminate in a written dissertation , regardless of degree designation . The music education degree can be a D.M.A. or Ph.D . Also , music education Ph.D . programs may include performance-oriented tracks . A related program is the Doctor of Sacred Music ( D.S.M. ) which tends to be awarded by seminaries or university music schools that focus on church music , choral conducting and organ performance . # Components # D.M.A. students typically complete applied studies , such as lessons or mentoring with a professor , and take courses within their area of specialization . In many D.M.A. programs , all of the different D.M.A. streams ( e.g. , performance , composition , conducting ) take a common core of music theory and music history courses . Many D.M.A. programs require students to pass a comprehensive exam on their area of specialization and on subjects such as music history and music theory .
