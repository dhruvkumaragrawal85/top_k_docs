Simple living and self-sufficiency were Thoreau 's other goals , and the whole project was inspired by transcendentalist philosophy , a central theme of the American Romantic Period . As Thoreau made clear in his book , his cabin was not in wilderness but at the edge of town , about two miles ( 3 km ) from his family home .
