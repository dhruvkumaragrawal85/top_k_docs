Its candidate at the presidential elections , Gonzalo Snchez de Lozada , won 22.5% of the popular vote and was subsequently confirmed by parliament as president . After the 2002 elections , it ruled in a coalition with the Revolutionary Left Movement , with Snchez as president ( he had been president from 1993 to 1997 ) . Snchez was overthrown in a 2003 popular uprising , and the party 's future is uncertain , especially as its last government has been tarnished by serious accusations of corruption , mismanagement , and even homicidal repression .
