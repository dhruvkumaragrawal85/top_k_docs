All crew and passengers were killed with the exception of a 4-year-old girl , Cecelia Cichan. * On December 27 , 1991 , SAS Flight 751 , an MD-81 OY-KHO Dana Viking crash-landed at Gottrra , Sweden . In the initial climb , both engines ingested ice broken loose from the wings ( which had not been properly de-iced before departure ) . The ice damaged the compressor blades causing compressor stall . The stall further caused repeated engine surges that finally destroyed both engines , leaving the aircraft with no propulsion . The aircraft landed in a snowy field and broke in three parts .
