When Hardin 's band got a job at the Dreamland Caf in Chicago , the following year , she prepared for Louis ' return to Chicago by having a huge banner made to advertise him as The World 's Greatest Trumpet Player . Louis was gaining an impressive reputation when Richard M. Jones convinced Okeh Records to make a series of sessions under his name : the classic Armstrong Hot Five recordings . With Hardin at the piano , Kid Ory on trombone , Johnny Dodds on clarinet , and Johnny St. Cyr on banjo , this stellar group rehearsed at Louis and Hardin 's residence on Chicago 's East 41st Street and held its first session on November 15 , 1925 . Few recordings are as celebrated as the ones made by the Hot Five ( and , sometimes , with Earl Hines replacing Hardin , the Hot Seven ) between then and the end of 1928 .
