The population was 1,211 at the 2000 census . The town is served by the Belle Vernon Area School District , whose buildings are located in adjacent Rostraver Township , Westmoreland County . Bellevernon , as it was originally spelled , was laid out in 1813 by Noah Speer in northwestern Fayette County . French for beautiful green , this was the name chosen by both Noah Speer for his little community on the Monongahela River and later by his son Louis , who founded a town with nearly the same name just up the hill ( North Belle Vernon ) . Noah Speer was the father of Louis M. Speer , Esq. , born in a log cabin on the Speer homestead/Gibsonton farm in 1810 . The younger Mr. Speer was quite the business man and played a large role in the development of Belle Vernon .
