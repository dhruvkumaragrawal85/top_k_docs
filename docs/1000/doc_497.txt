Some well-known examples are the King 's Gambit ( 1. e4 e5 2. f4 ) , Queen 's Gambit ( 1. d4 d5 2. c4 ) , and Evans Gambit ( 1. e4 e5 2 . Nf3 Nc6 3 . Bc4 Bc5 4. b4 ) .
