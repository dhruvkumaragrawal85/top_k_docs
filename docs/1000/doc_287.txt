The highest temperature was 39.0 C , observed on 17 November 1985 . The highest cumulative rainfall recorded in 24 hours in the city between June 1988 and October 2008 was 143.4 mm in 25 days May 2005 . Between 1890 and 2004 there were 41 occurrences of frost in Campinas . The most recent was on July 18 , 2000 , when the minimum temperature reached 2.2 C. There are also occasional episodes of strong winds , with gusts exceeding 100 km / h , and training records were made in the city day May 4 , 2001 and March 9 , 2008 . The wet season is from mid-October to mid-April , with heavier rains particularly in December , January , February and early March , and the dry season is from mid-May to mid-September .
