Its climate is almost the same as Cairo 's , owing to its proximity . Wind storms can be frequent across Egypt in spring , bringing Saharan dust into the city during the months of March and April . High temperatures in winter range from 16C 0 to 20C 0 , while nighttime lows drop to below 7C 0 . In summer , the highs rarely surpass 40C 0 , and the lows can drop to about 20C 0 . Rainfall is sparse , but sudden showers do cause harsh flooding .
